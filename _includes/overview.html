Overview

Holmes Processing was born out of the need to rapidly process and analyze large volumes of threat intelligence data. The system creates a distributed architecture that allows the system to scale horizontally while also providing the flexible needed to evolve to the ever changing landscape of computer security. Whether you have thousands or millions of objects, Holmes Processing can easily help you extract useful information and generate meaningful intelligence.

Extract

Execute static and dynamic analysis techniques and also query 3rd parties for information.


Store

Centrally manage raw data, extracted information, and generated intelligence.


Analyze

Provides the infrastructure required to perform statistical, machine learning, and other analytic methods.
